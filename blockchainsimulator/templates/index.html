What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a decentralized and distributed ledger that records transactions across many computers in such a way that the registered transactions cannot be altered retroactively without the alteration of all subsequent blocks.

How This Simulator Works

This simulator mimics a blockchain, allowing you to mine new blocks and view the current blockchain. The process of mining involves finding a proof of work, which requires computational power.

  • Mining: Add new blocks to the blockchain.
  • View Blockchain: View the entire list of blocks in the blockchain.
